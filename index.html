Biking - Health Benefits

To be fit and healthy you need to be physically active. Regular physical activity can help protect you from serious diseases such as obesity, heart disease, cancer, mental illness, diabetes and arthritis. Riding your bicycle regularly is one of the best ways to reduce your risk of health problems associated with a sedentary lifestyle.

Things to remember

Biking can help to protect you from serious diseases such as stroke, heart attack, some cancers, depression, diabetes, obesity and arthritis. However, riding a bike requires proper gears and tip to be safe and fun. You need to wear all the necessary safety gears and obey traffic rules while biking
